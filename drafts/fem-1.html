Introduction

Finite Element Method is a technique for obtaining numerical solution to differential equations. A physical problem is expressed in the form of a governing differential equation.

Classical Stiffness Method

The Classical Stiffness Method was developed for solving structural analysis problems in civil engineering. It does not involve differential equations.
