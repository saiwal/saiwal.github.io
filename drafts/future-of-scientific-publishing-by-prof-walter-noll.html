Future of Scientific Publishing by Prof. Walter Noll

Published: Wed 04 July 2018
By Saiwal

In Academia.

tags: research publishing

In the age of internet when information travels at lightning fast speeds, the process of publishing a scientific article remains the same, often the process lasting for months. In the essay linked below, Prof. Walter Noll outlines a simpler, more efficient and perhaps a better way of communicating scientific ideas.
