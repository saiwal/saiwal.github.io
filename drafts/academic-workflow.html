    Wed 08 February 2017 Academia research / software

    Organizing your worklow is very essential to maintain easy access to information and not losing track of yur work files. There are many ways of organizing your workflow, however from an academic point of view it is also necessary to organize your notes, assignments, ebooks and other documents chronologically. This makes it convinient for you to revisit any course you have taken or if you smply need to access the registration document from first semester.

    What are you organizing

    The first step is identifying the documents/files that you are going to deal with. I prefer not to include any big files such as videos as this creates a problem if you're trying to backup online and your space quota runs out. Pdfs, ebooks, images are fine.
