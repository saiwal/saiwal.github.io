  • What does it mean to have a PhD
    Thu 13 August 2015 Academia research / PhD / education

    Having a PhD means that you have demonstrated your ability to master a field and you have up-to-date knowledge in that field. It does not restrict you to a particular field rather it demonstrates your ability to take any problem at hand and solve it using a systematic scientific approach.

    - Saiwal Krishna
