    Basic equation of Motion

    A spring mass system with an additional damper can be cconsidered to be the simplest single-degree-of-freedom system. The free body diagram for the mass in motion can be constructed as shown___. The forces acting are

    \[ F_{I} = m\ddot{u} \]

    \[ F_{S} = ku \]

    \[ F_{D} = c\dot{u} \]

    where $ F_I $ is inertial force acting on the mass, $ F_S $ is the spring force acting on the mass and $ F_D $ is the damper force acting on mass because of its velocity.

    Summing up all the forces, for equilibrium we get,

    \[ m\ddot{u} + c\dot{u} + ku = 0 \]

    which is the basic equation of motion for an oscillating body.
